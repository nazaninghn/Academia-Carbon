Understanding Scopes

Scope 1 - Direct Emissions

Emissions from sources you own or control

  • Company vehicles
  • Boilers and furnaces
  • Refrigerant leaks

Scope 2 - Energy Indirect

Emissions from purchased energy

  • Electricity consumption
  • District heating/cooling
  • Purchased steam

Scope 3 - Other Indirect

All other indirect emissions

  • Business travel
  • Employee commuting
  • Waste disposal
  • Purchased goods

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the emission factors?
All emission factors are based on international standards (IPCC, EPA, DEFRA) and country-specific data from official sources like IEA and Turkish Ministry of Environment. Turkey-specific factors are updated annually.
Can I change the country after calculating?
No, once a calculation is saved, the country cannot be changed. Always select your country before calculating. You can delete the record and recalculate if needed.
What if my country is not listed?
Use "Global Average" which provides internationally recognized emission factors. We're continuously adding more countries based on user demand.
How often should I track emissions?
We recommend monthly tracking for Scope 1 and 2 (electricity, fuel), and quarterly for Scope 3 (travel, waste). This provides good balance between accuracy and effort.
